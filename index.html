应用简介：

ESA阅卷是一款面向阅卷老师的移动端云端阅卷应用，让老师从繁琐的线下传统阅卷解脱出来，轻松批阅试卷。阅卷数据信息化，有助于老师把控阅卷质量。

产品优势：
1、随时随地轻松批阅试卷
2、实时统计阅卷进度及各个题块的阅卷分数情况
3、支持随时回评试卷修改分数
4、对考生试卷支持多种阅卷处理，包括试评、正评、仲裁卷处理、问题卷处理

Description：

ESA Marking is a mobile cloud scoring application for scoring teachers, allowing teachers to free themselves from traditional offline marking and mark papers easily. Informatization of marking data helps teachers to control the quality of marking.

Advantages：
1、Easily marking at any time and place
2、Real-time follow-up of the progress of marking and the marking scores of each question block
3、Support to review paper and revise score at any time
4、Supports multiple marking processing, including trial marking, formal marking and arbitration paper processing
